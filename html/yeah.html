Alpine Forest Soap

Alpine Forest Soap is cold process soap that is all-natural, handcrafted soap designed to cleanse and nourish all skin types. Alpine Forest Soap is infused with Unrefined Yellow Shea Butter to promote healthy, soft, and silky-smooth skin. Scrublands Apothecary uses two ounces of Unrefined Yellow Shea Butter per pound of soap. Cedarwood Essential Oil is added to the mix to help clean and soothe the skin. The cold process soap making method is used to create a dense, long lasting soap bar that locks in the aroma of the essential oils. All Scrublands Apothecary Soap is cold process soap made in the back country of North Carolina. Scrublands Apothecary will guarantee you are 100% satisfied with your purchase or your money back.
