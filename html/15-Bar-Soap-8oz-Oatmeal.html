8oz Ground Oatmeal Soap

X

The Ground Oats and Honey Soap is all-natural, homemade soap formulated to soothe and nourish the skin. Whole Milk contains omega essential fatty acids, amino acids, and proteins to fortify the skin against damage. Unrefined Yellow Shea Butter is added to the soap to create a moisturizing skin barrier to lock water inside the skin. Organic Steel Cut Oatmeal Oats are added to soothe the skin. Organic honey is added as a humectant to draw water to the skin. The beautiful golden brown soap bar is infused with Oatmeal and Honey Fragrance Oil.


The cold process soap making method ensures that the ground oatmeal oats, shea butter, and organic honey are preserved during the soap making process. The cold process soap making method creates a dense, long lasting soap bar that locks in moisturizing glycerin and nourishing fatty acids.


** Allergy Warning: People with skin sensitivities or allergies should test the soap on a small spot of the skin and stop using it if any irritation occurs. 


**No Detergents, No Sodium Lauryl Sulfate, No Harsh Chemicals, No Sulfates 


** Soap is shrink wrapped to protect and preserve the soap bar freshness.
