Pure-Castile Unscented Dog Shampoo is perfectly formulated for your four-legged friend. Your dog’s skin is important for their health. Dogs have sensitive skin, and it is important to use shampoo formulated for them. Homemade liquid soap is ideal for animals because your dog’s skin needs a higher pH Level identical to the pH level of homemade liquid soap.


We make liquid soap using potassium hydroxide, vegetable oils, vegetable glycerin, distilled water, and essential oils. The primary base oil used in the soap is Extra Virgin Olive Oil and this creates the soft, gentle lather that nourishes and protects your dog’s skin with vitamins and fatty acids. The coconut oil and palm oil enhance the cleansing properties of the soap making it possible to remove dirt and grim quickly and effectively. Castor oil intensifies the lather of the soap and helps protect and condition the skin. Vegetable Glycerin protects and conditions their skin leaving their fur coat soft and vibrant.

My Portuguese Water Dog runs like lightning and invariably finds the mud no matter how dry it is outside. When I bath them, I use Scrublands Apothecary's All-Natural Alpine Forest Dog Shampoo.
