Marine Green Sea Salt Soap

X

Marine Green Sea Salt Soap is a luxurious soap made with Himalayan Sea Salt, Shea Butter, and Eucalyptus Essential Oil. Marine Green Himalayan Sea Salt Soap is loaded with Himalayan Sea Salt. We use one cup of Himalayan Sea Salt per pound of cold process soap. Large size granules of Himalayan Sea Salt are added to induce a creamy lather that soothes and softens the skin. The sea salts create a solid, dense bar of soap that is gentle for sensitive skin types.  Unrefined Yellow Shea butter is added at 10% of the total base oils. Shea butter creates a moisturizing layer of protection for the skin and covers the body with all-natural vitamins and skin loving fatty acids. 


All Scrublands Apothecary Soap is cold process soap made in the backcountry of North Carolina. Scrublands Apothecary will guarantee you are %100 satisfied with your purchase or your money back.


** Allergy Warning: People with skin sensitivities or allergies should test the soap on a small spot of the skin and stop using it if any irritation occurs.


**No Detergents, No Sodium Lauryl Sulfate, No Harsh Chemicals, No Sulfates 


** Soap is shrink wrapped to protect and preserve the soap bar freshness.
